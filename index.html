Goodwill Capitalsim is a new economic experiment designed to leverage the latest advances in technology and cultural trends to upgrade Capitalism into a truly open and free system that will bring hope and opportunity to everyone. Goodwill Capitalism introduces a new social currency, called GeeCees (Goodwill Currency) which in combination with money will provide the perfect market mechanisms to minimize the social impact of economic crises. You can find more about it by looking it up on the web on my blog site.

In order to do a proof of concept we will try to simulate market behaviors in a GeeCee economy, by implementing a social currency exchange system in which the users can use an app or browser based system to earn or borrow social currency and share it with other users. They can also award GeeCees to each other - simulating exchange of goods and services and favors and mock a traditional commercial transaction. The system will enforce certain rules and regulations that will then become the guiding framework for the real system. We will use the results and feedback from this trial to determine the next steps w.r.t further development of the concept and generating an awarness campaign.

FAQ

Is Goodwill Capitalism a real system?
No. It is still in experimental and theortical stage. This is a mock trial to see how it is accepted by general public
What will this trial proove?
Goodwill Capitalism is all about changing market behaviors. This trail will test one such behavior, among many others. Namely, How do people respond when their social values and civic behavior and productivity in the marketplace are appreciated and monetized.
